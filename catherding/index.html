Cat Herders

Nine cats have escaped into the park. Theyâ€™re grumpy and would prefer you leave them alone. Nonetheless, you employ a team of animal control agents and your job is to round them up. You win by nabbing five cats.

Turn Sequence

  1. Click/Tap to move your red (player 1) or blue (player 2) herders.
  2. After moving a herder, you choose where the cats run off to.
  3. You catch a cat (and score) if it is next to two or more of your herders after it tries to escape.
